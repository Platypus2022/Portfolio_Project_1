Forty Fathom Freedivers

A club for relaxed cold-water divers and

Like-minded people with a love for the sea

Come to the deep waters where life began and discover your inner fish


Relish the beauty of the sea's flora and fauna


Learn techniques that will improve your quality of life


We are a group of free-divers who reguarly meet at the many craggy coves

off West-Cork. Our goal is to promote the sport of free-diving and enhance

its popularity.


We are entirely non-profit, we do it for the fun, the health and the social

aspect.
